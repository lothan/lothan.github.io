- _The Ghostwriter_ (2010)
  - Perfectly cast movie. For some reason I thought this was set/made earlier, maybe because it looks terrible. Really made me believe the war on terror was all the thought up by long island guys who wear bowties.
- _Kung Fu Hustle_ (2004)
  - Good movie to watch when sick - you're not thinking straight and want to laugh, but not like, out loud.
- _Mission: Impossible - The Final Reckoning_ (2025)
  - I liked it when the bad guy said to Tom Cruise: "This is the Final Reckoning." A 3-hr clip show that was almost unwatchable except for the submarine and biplane scenes, which were excellent.
